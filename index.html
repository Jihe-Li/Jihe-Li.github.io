About

I am currently a PhD student in the Medical Image Analysis (MIA) Lab at the ARTORG Center for Biomedical Engineering Research, University of Bern, Switzerland, supervised by Prof. Mauricio Reyes. Additionally, I am fortunate to work closely with Dr. Xia Li at ETH Zurich.

Prior to this, I obtained my Master's degree from Peking University, China (June 2025) and my Bachelor's degree from Jilin University, China (June 2022).

My research interests focus on exploring AI techniques for radiotherapy, including dosimetry- and toxicity-aware image segmentation, AI-based Treatment Planning Systems (TPS), and deformable image registration.
